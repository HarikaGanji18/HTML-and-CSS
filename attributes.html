Doraemon

Doraemon is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Fujiko F. Fujio1. The story revolves around a robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to aid a boy named Nobita Nobi1. The name "Doraemon" roughly translates to "stray" and is written in a mixture of two Japanese scripts: Katakana and Hiragana2. Doraemon is a big deal in Japanese culture and is often compared to Mickey Mouse3.
